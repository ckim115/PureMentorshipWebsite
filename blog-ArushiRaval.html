Intern Letter: Arushi Raval

Hi!


My name is Arushi and I’m the social media intern here at Pure Mentorship working with others as part of the branding and marketing team.


My experience has been very enjoyable and diving into the world of media and all that comes with it has been incredibly eye-opening.


From learning about ways to curate a meaningful presence to using analytics to grow the brand, I have learned so much in just a couple of weeks!


The opportunity to network with people I otherwise would have never met has been really cool. The Pure Mentorship Team is very friendly and easy to communicate with, fostering a safe and innovative environment.


I can’t wait to see what the rest of my internship has to offer!
