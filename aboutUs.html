Our Mission

Pure Mentorship strives to uplift students across the country to pursue otherwise inaccessible careers in technology, engineering, and management. We firmly believe in the importance of mentorship in helping individuals achieve their goals and reach their full potential.

Meet the team!

Meet the hardworking team behind Pure Mentorship! Our team is 21 members strong, consisting of students across different backgrounds with a shared passion for technology, leadership, and most importantly, mentorship. We all had various mentors growing up, and we are excited to pay this act of kindness forward to students around the country.

A Letter from our Founder:

Hello! My name is Thenu and I am a nationally-recognized leader and the founder of Pure Mentorship! I strongly believe in the potential of each individual and know that support from a mentor at a young age can propel them toward a lifetime of success. I created Pure Mentorship and the mission is simple. We bring together successful and well-rounded mentors and mentees who are seeking help in various areas of their lives whether they are in high school, early college, or early career. We are based out of San Jose, CA and our mentors come from all over the United States.

Our mentors are working in various fields including marketing, aerospace, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and computer science. They come from schools just like yours and are pursuing higher education at local universities as well as ivy league colleges. Now they are working at companies such as Lockheed Martin Space, Amazon, Palo Alto Networks, NVIDIA, or their own startup! They are passionate about supporting youth individuals through their goals no matter how big or how small.

My best,

Thenu S.
